INTERNSHIP

INNER

BUDDIES

• 01 •

During my second year of studies I had the chance to go on an internship and feel what is like to be working in the field of ICT and get to know the atmosphere within we would find ourselves (hopefully) in the near future. The core of the project that I worked on was to give the official website of Inner Buddies redesigned look. The purpose of the redesign was not only to raise the popularity of the company, therefore making it more successful, but also intrigue the people, current customers, and newcomers, and raise their awareness of the people’s health, and more particularly their gut health – the microbiome flora and fauna.

Visit the live version of Inner Buddies' website

Client

Inner Buddies

Year

2022-2023

My Role

Lead Designer & Front-end Developer

The Issue

The majority of youth are spending significant amounts of time online, with many spending over three hours a day and a significant portion of them having online contact for the entire day. This means that online communication is important for reaching the youth. While digital youth work, such as gaming events and programming together, is already a popular activity, online youth work is still largely non-existent. because of this, many youth organisations in eindhoven cannot connect to all members of their target group and help them

Research Question/s

“What raises the user's awareness about their health and especially their microbiome flora?”

“How would we show the users that they can rely on Inner Buddies services?”

“How would we manage to give Inner Buddies' website a renewed look while keeping the spirit of the company as it is now?”
