SEMESTER PROJECT

NETFLIX

LOYALTY PROGRAM

• 01 •

During my studies I had the opportunity to work in a collaboration with the famous e-sports team "Liquid". We had to work in a collaboration, coming up and developing a concept for a loyalty program for a company of our choice. My team and I chose to create and design a loyalty platform for the famous online streaming giant Netflix. That is where the Netflix X was born.

Visit the concept's final prototype

Client

Team Liquid, Netflix

Year

2021-2022

My Role

Lead Designer & Front-end Developer

The Issue

Apart from that, Netflix has created a distinguished and widely known face and reputation, that users instantly recognize just by looking at the colours, the creation of such loyalty platform would have brought only more benefit for the company itself.

Why we chose to design and develop such a platform was to give back to the fans - when a person feels as a part of a community, they are more likely to stay and share with others. Thus, by saying “Thank you” to the already existing users, they are more likely to come back and as excited as the first time they joined the family of Netflix.

Research Question/s

"How could we help the company of Netflix, to not only keep its popularity as the most famous streaming platform, but also continue to grow and see a rise in their revenue and interest rate?"

• 02 •

The purpose of was project to contribute to the Netflix community by providing them with Netflix X - platform specifically designed for giving back to the loyal customers who appreciate the core values of the Netflix. Working over such a topic, together with such a collaborator did not only allowed me to get in touch with professionals from the field but also gave me the "spark" and desire to continue my growth as an UX/UI Designer.
