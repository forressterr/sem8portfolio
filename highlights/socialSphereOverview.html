SEMESTER PROJECT

SOCIAL

SPHERE

"Your hub for mastering social media with awareness"

• 01 •

Social Sphere is a concept for an online educational platform, where youth workers can learn how to use various social media sites, in order to connect easily to the youth.

The main features of the platform will be learning lessons, completing quests, and acquiring rewards, certificates of mastery after every finished course, option to choose specific social media or have an archetype. Currently, based on our research findings, the 4 included social media platforms are Instagram, Snapchat, TikTok and Discord.

Visit the concept's final prototype

Client

Garage 2020, Municipality of Eindhoven

Year

2023

My Role

Lead Designer - UX/UI Design

The Issue

The majority of youth are spending significant amounts of time online, with many spending over three hours a day and a significant portion of them having online contact for the entire day. This means that online communication is important for reaching the youth. While digital youth work, such as gaming events and programming together, is already a popular activity, online youth work is still largely non-existent. because of this, many youth organisations in eindhoven cannot connect to all members of their target group and help them

Research Question

“How can social workers from Eindhoven relate and connect to the online world of youth while countering the cons of online media?”

• 02 •

An international student team collaborated with Garage2020 to create an online platform for youth workers in Eindhoven. They followed the Design Thinking methodology through three phases: Research, Design, and Testing.

The research phase involved studying youth work extensively, though time constraints led to some uncertainties. Despite challenges, the research laid a solid foundation.

The design phase, while enjoyable, had its difficulties in finalizing the project's design and selecting typefaces. Nevertheless, the team created a visually captivating platform.

In the validation phase, they sought feedback from stakeholders and experts to ensure their project met its goals, overcoming challenges like coordinating with test participants. The team faced various difficulties throughout, such as falling behind schedule and missing team members. This experience taught them the importance of professional research, making suitable design choices, and enhancing social skills.

Overall, the project allowed for personal growth, emphasizing the value of teamwork and resilience in tackling challenges.
